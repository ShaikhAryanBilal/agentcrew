## Trigger
Campaign brief / content calendar / brand guidelines available.
## Instructions
1. Plan — define goals, audience, channel mix, budget allocation
 2. Creative — brief designers for ad creative across channels
 3. Launch — set up campaign tracking, UTMs, conversion pixels, launch
 4. Optimize — monitor performance, adjust bids, pause underperformers
 5. Report — compile attribution report, ROAS analysis, optimization recommendations

6. Log to .crewmarkdown/logs/<yyyy>/<MM>/<dd>/<HHmmss>/<chat-slug>-<HHmmss>/marketing-performance.md
7. Update .crewmarkdown/state/workflow.json
## Done When
Campaign assets produced, channels activated, KPIs tracking.
